Breakpoints detection

We divided time-series in segments separated by breakpoints

  • Segements correspond to stable land use regimes
  • Breakpoints correspond to shifts in land use regime
    • Stable forest to agriculture (Burning)
    • Agriculture to regrowing forest (Abandonment)
    • Regrowing forest to stable forest (Stabilization)

Profiles characterization by classification of breakpoints

Now we have segmented time-series at field level, but this is still very raw information that needs to be characterized
  • Number of burn events correspond to number of slash and burn cycles
  • Visual interpretation of (300?) breakpoints
  • Training of random forest classifier

      • Regressors are:

    • Characteristics of surrounding segments
  • Classification of breakpoints
  • Number of burning breakpoints == number of cultivation cycles
